Data

Much of the data we used for model building and testing came from product review videos on YouTube.

We did leverage some publicly available Amazon review data......

The data is provided by Julian McAuley from the University of California, San Diego. His site can be found here. http://jmcauley.ucsd.edu/data/amazon/

We specifically used data from the 2018 page and limited the review dates to 2015 - 2018. Cameras, electronics, and phone data were used. We first identified the product ID's within a category for a given product category, e.g. phones, then filtered the reviews according to the product ID. In the case of phones, for example, there were thousands of product IDs which yielded a training dataset of over 300K+ data points.
